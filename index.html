Let's Get in Touch

Topic analysis of Venmo feed

The goal of this project was to see if we could draw insights into consumer spending behavior -- given a person or group of people, could we identify the types of transactions that they use Venmo for?

By scraping Venmo’s public transaction feed I was able to capture information on all public transactions taking place within the app and perform LDA topic analysis on the transaction descriptions.

Utilizing AWS for facial recognition was a cool add-in for how you could query the database and get transaction insight into a specific user.

Check it out on GitHub

Automating Roster Transactions

Everyone who plays fantasy football knows that good teams are made in the draft but great teams are made on the waiver wire.

In the league I play in with my friends from school, we utilize rolling waivers for the course of the season rather than a system that resets each week based off of league standings. Because of this, owners with good position in the queue are inclined to save their waiver claims and would rather try to pick up players as soon as they hit free agency right after waivers process each week.

Wanting to be competitive but not wanting to stay up until 4:30 AM when waivers process, I decided to write a script to automate this process utilizing Selenium and Cron automation.

Check it out on GitHub
